Running an FFL dealership means managing ATF compliance, POS, inventory, customer communications, and eCommerce — often across separate tools that don't talk to each other. The right software can mean the difference between a clean ATF inspection and a citation. Here's an honest look at your options in 2026.

⚠️
Disclosure: We built BoundStack, so we're not a neutral party. We've done our best to represent competitors fairly based on public reviews, forum discussions, and dealer feedback — but you should test all options yourself.

What to Look for in FFL Compliance Software

Before comparing specific products, here's what actually matters for your operation:

  • ATF compliance features — bound book (A&D), e4473, auto-3310.4. These are non-negotiable.
  • Reliability — crashes during busy periods (gun shows, weekends) cost you sales and create compliance gaps.
  • Support responsiveness — when the IOI shows up, you need someone who picks up the phone.
  • Modern UI — if your staff dreads using it, they'll make errors.
  • All-in-one vs. point solution — do you want one tool or five?
  • 2A alignment — will this company still exist and serve you in 5 years?
  • Free trial — every serious competitor offers one. Never buy without testing.

FastBound

FastBound
Most Popular
From ~$79/mo · 14-day free trial

FastBound is the market leader in FFL compliance software, used by thousands of dealers across the country. It focuses exclusively on the compliance side — bound book, e4473, and NICS — and does it well. It's been around since 2013 and has deep trust in the community.

Pros

  • Excellent 4.8/5 rating on Capterra
  • Very reliable uptime track record
  • Strong ATF compliance features
  • Good customer support
  • 14-day free trial
  • Trusted by the community for 10+ years

Cons

  • Dated interface — dealers note "learning curve with layout"
  • Compliance only — no POS, inventory, or eCommerce
  • You still need 3-4 other tools, adding $200-400/mo
  • No all-in-one option
Best for: Dealers who want a proven, compliance-only solution and are happy managing separate POS and inventory tools.

FFLSafe

FFLSafe
Free
Free · Desktop only

FFLSafe offers a free electronic bound book and is the only competitor praised for having a clean, modern UI. For dealers on a tight budget who just need basic A&D record keeping, it's a legitimate option.

Pros

  • Completely free
  • Best UI of any legacy product
  • Simple to get started

Cons

  • Desktop only — no mobile, no cloud
  • No e4473
  • No POS, inventory, or eCommerce
  • Limited support
  • If your computer dies, so does your data
Best for: Kitchen table FFLs doing very low volume who just need a free bound book and aren't worried about cloud access or e4473.

Orchid / Coreware (Legacy POS)

Orchid / Coreware
From ~$150-300/mo

These are the legacy all-in-one platforms — they try to do everything (compliance + POS + inventory) but are built on old technology stacks. Reviews are mixed at best.

Pros

  • Attempts all-in-one coverage
  • Established brands with history

Cons

  • Coreware rated 1.8/5 on SourceForge
  • "Over-promising and under-delivering" per dealers
  • Broken features that were sold but never worked
  • Support goes silent on unresolved tickets
  • Outdated UI that slows staff down
  • High price for the quality delivered
Best for: Shops already locked in with years of historical data who are afraid of migration risk. Otherwise, hard to recommend.

Which FFL Software Should You Choose?

There's no universal answer — it depends on your situation:

Choose FastBound if:

  • You want compliance-only and are happy managing separate POS/inventory tools
  • You value a long track record and large community above all else
  • You're already comfortable with their UI and don't want to switch

Choose FFLSafe if:

  • You're a kitchen table FFL doing very low volume
  • Budget is the #1 constraint and you don't need e4473 or cloud access

Choose BoundStack if:

  • You're tired of paying for 4-5 separate tools and manually re-entering data between them
  • You want a modern, cloud-native platform that works on your phone at a gun show
  • You want AI-powered compliance checks that catch errors before your inspection
  • You want to start free and scale as your operation grows
✅
