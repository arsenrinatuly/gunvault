The problem

Every FFL Dealer
Knows This Pain.

Paper A&D books get lost
ATF inspectors aren't forgiving. Handwritten records, illegible entries, missing disposition dates — violations stack fast.
4473 forms take forever
Manual paper forms, retyping info, waiting on fax confirmations. Your customer is standing at the counter for 45 minutes.
Legacy software costs $300+/mo
Built for distributors, priced for distributors. Small shops pay enterprise prices for features they never use.
Payment processors drop FFLs
Stripe, Square, PayPal — all have de-platformed gun shops. One policy change and your POS is dead.
No internet = no records
Gun shows, remote ranges, spotty rural service. If your software needs the cloud, your business stops without it.
Multi-handgun reports missed
ATF 3310.4 requires reporting two+ handguns to the same buyer within 5 days. Missing it is a federal violation.

Digital A&D Bound Book
